Frequently Asked Questions

At Baker Exteriors, we want to take the guessing game out of exterior repair and renovation. If you are new to a project, you probably have some questions, and hopefully, we can answer them! Here are some of our most frequently asked questions (and remember, no question is too small. If you're not sure, ask us!)

FAQ's

Q: How do I claim damage to my home's exterior with my insurance company?

A: Let us walk you through it...

First, we will come to your house and inspect for damage. This is our FREE, no pressure home evaluation.

Next, you can contact your insurance company to initiate the claim process. When you have a date scheduled for the adjuster to come by, let us know and we will be there.

Then, the restoration process begins! You can be present for the Adjuster meeting or not, it is up to you. We keep you informed of insurance decisions during this stage, so you will never be in the dark.

Next, once your insurance company approves the project, the Adjuster will request a payment to be made out to you from your insurance company-for about half the cost of the project. We will come back over and help you decide on details like colors and shingles. We will sign a contract for you to have a copy so we are all on the same page! At this point, we collect the first payment.

Once your choices have been made, we will order your materials. When they are delivered, we inspect them for quality.

Now, construction begins! Our crew and project manager will begin your home's transformation. We will give you details of the project up front so you know what to expect. Most houses take between 1-2 days for an entire roof install.

When we complete construction on your home, we will walk through with you and check all aspects of our work to be sure you are completely satisfied.

Finally, we will invoice your insurance company for the remaining payment on your project, to be sent to you. Once you receive the payment, you can send it to us, or we can come by to pick it up and check in on you and your home. We will then provide you with a lien waiver and your warranty. Your satisfaction is our number one priority!

Q: I don't know what style my home is, or how to improve it. Do you have any recommendations?

A: Absolutely! We would be happy to provide design assistance on any project you dream up-at no cost!

Q: Are you locally based?

A: We are! Located in O'Fallon, MO, but servicing all of Eastern Missouri, you can be assured our family-owned business will be here, taking care of our neighbors for years to come.
